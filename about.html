About Us

Who are we

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises, LLC is a global trading company with two branch locations: our headquarters in Phoenix, USA, and a branch office in Madrid, Spain. Our organization is proud to be a 100% Veteran owned and operated small business, serving large and small clients in both commercial and government sectors. We specialize in efficiently importing and exporting products and brokering international and domestic trade agreements. Our organization provides additional unique services, including government contract procurement & fulfillment, trade contract negotiation, and freight broker services.

To date, PWE has established invaluable associations in international business with ongoing operations in Latin America, the Caribbean, European countries, and West African markets.

What drives us

Excellence and customer satisfaction is the pillar of what we do. Whether working directly with manufacturers to expand their global market presence, securing a diverse range of products for our buyers, or importing & exporting goods for our partners or retail locations, our core competencies and business philosophy never waivers.

Lifelong relationships

We are a group of young and adventurous entrepreneurs with invaluable international experience that has brought us to all corners of the globe. Our love for cultural diversity and exploring worldwide markets has helped us develop a collection of long-lasting, influential relationships. Don't hesitate to contact our team if you are interested in joining our professional network or inquiring about services.

Meet the founders

Cristiane Scott Pineda

Co-Founder & Managing Director of International Trade and Operations

Our partner Cristiane Pineda served in the United States Marine Corps and Operation Enduring Freedom before transitioning out of the armed services and into the technology sector. After completing his undergraduate program at CSU Fresno, Cris attended a graduate program at the renowned IE University in Spain. He then worked as a data scientist, where he procured years of experience applying his data analytics and technology expertise to international business and building sustainable client relations.

Cristiane has further established himself in the global business sector through his unwavering knowledge of various cultures and languages acquired through traveling to over 40 countries and collecting a robust international network of clients and professionals. Being multilingual/multicultural with a diverse educational background, Cristiane equips Pineda & Wiley Enterprises to recognize and convey potential cultural sensitivities to our partners and provide our clients with a more personal and unique experience. He has a deep passion for building lasting relationships and going above and beyond to fulfill our client's demands. He currently oversees International Relations and Trade Operations from our branch office in Madrid, Spain.

Adam Ryan Wiley

Co-Founder & Managing Director of Government Services and Finance

Our partner Adam Wiley served in the United States Marine Corps, and Operation Inherent Resolve before honorably transitioning out of the armed services and into the technology and analytics sector. After completing his undergraduate program at ASU Tempe in Data Analytics, Adam began working as an analytics consultant for one of the world's four largest professional services networks. While in this role, he applied exceptional risk analysis, assessment support, and analytical solutions to clients across the Consumer Goods, Energy, Resources & Industries, and Financial Services industries.

Adam continues to employ his passion for leveraging the power of meaningful data by uncovering solutions to challenging international trade and logistics constraints. Adam's global experience and risk assessment approach provides Pineda & Wiley Enterprises the unique ability to accurately evaluate transactions for our clients, improve their supply chain and connect businesses to markets that expand by profitability. He currently oversees our Federal, State, & Local Government Contracting Services and Risk & Financial Operations from our branch HQ in Phoenix, USA.
