Import & Export

Our Mission: Import and export commodity goods and services worldwide.

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises facilitates the importation and exportation of various products spanning from the automotive industry, including vehicles and automotive parts, all the way down to the clothing and garment industry. We currently operate as the premier export trading company for domestic and international companies in the automotive, clothing manufacturing, and healthcare product sectors. PwE has established, unparalleled, networking abilities for profitable and personable international business through our exceptional international relationships.
