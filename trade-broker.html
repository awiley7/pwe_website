International Trade Broker

Our Mission: Provide intermediary services between global suppliers to connect their products with international markets to satisfy their interests more efficiently and expand their worldwide reach.

Our Trade Broker services help global suppliers and buyers break into emerging or existing markets through our comprehensive business relationships in various countries and industry knowledge in international trade. Our mission statement is rooted in market analysis, a people-first approach, and leveraging our global professional network to methodically broker profitable transactions that minimize the impacts of cultural differences and trade practices.

PWE has established invaluable associations in international business with ongoing operations in Latin America, the Caribbean, European countries, and West African markets.

We represent exceptional manufacturers within the following consumer goods categories. All our manufacturers have been vetted and certified through our risk assessment process to ensure ethicality and reliability.

Fruits & Vegetables

Our partners offer a comprehensive portfolio of fruits & vegetables from premier growing regions worldwide.

We work directly with suppliers of Tahiti Limes, Mangos, and other products from Latin America and West Africa.

Coffee

There is no better source for distinguished, exclusive coffee strains than Latin America's top coffee bean-growing regions.

Our export-ready partners offer Arabica, Robusta, and Excelsa variants ranging from commercial to premium.

Premier Tobacco

We work with award-winning and globally renowned cigar manufacturers in the Caribbean.

The Dominican Republic is known for having the best conditions for cultivating tobacco, allowing us to deliver a wide range of premium and commercial cigars manufactured in the world's cigar capital, Santiago, Dominican Republic.

Seafood

Seafood is enjoyed worldwide, but it can often be challenging to obtain quality seafood products.

Our partners can deliver frozen or packaged products from the Americas regardless of your geographical location. Please inquire about an updated seasonal inventory of seafood products.

Natural Resources

Our team works with Thermal Coal, Plastics suppliers, and other resources.

Please inquire for comprehensive list.

Other

We invite all clients to inquire about custom product requests for products not currently outlined. Our global professional network allows us to locate and source quality and reliable goods upon request.

Additionally, we often have limited flash sale items or seasonal rotating products, anything from containers of souvenirs, surgical masks, vegetable oils, and more. Contact us for updates or requests.
