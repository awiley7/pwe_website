Government Services

The U.S. Federal Government is the largest purchaser of American goods and services. As a Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB), we are 100% committed to helping fulfill the needs of our federal, state and local governments.

Our Mission Statement

The Pineda & Wiley Enterprises Government Services line is honored to be a Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB) and to have the opportunity to apply for and execute government contracts. Having both honorably served in the Marine Corps, our founders have a unique passion that allows for service long after hanging up the uniform. In doing so, they are able to deliver exceptional service to our country's critical needs. We understand the rapidly changing demands and commodity requirements necessary to operate efficiently at a high level. As a VOSB, we can deliver commodity and logistical needs while serving as a supervisory and scheduling intermediary with local contractors. This partnership allows you to consolidate all your suppliers and contractors under Pineda & Wiley Enterprises as a VOSB.
