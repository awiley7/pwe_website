Who We Are

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises, LLC is a global trading company with two branch locations: our headquarters in Phoenix, USA, and a branch office in Madrid, Spain. Our organization is proud to be a 100% Veteran owned and operated small business, serving large and small clients in both commercial and government sectors. We specialize in efficiently importing and exporting products and brokering international and domestic trade agreements. Our organization provides additional unique services, including government contract procurement & fulfillment, trade contract negotiation, and freight broker services.
