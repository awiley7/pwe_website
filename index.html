Who we are

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises, LLC is an American boutique international trading company that specializes in imports, exports, trade broker services and government services.

Our focus does not stop at providing exceptional service to our clients. We are also passionate about forging strong relationships, both international and domestic. We are a multi-national company currently operating in both the United States and Spain.

We are headquartered in the United States in Tempe, Arizona. We are 100% Veteran Owned and Operated.
