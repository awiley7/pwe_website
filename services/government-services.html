Government Services

Our Mission: The U.S. Federal Government is the largest purchaser of goods and services. As a Veteran- Owned Small Business (VOSB), we are 100% committed to helping fulfill the needs of our federal, state, and local governments.

The Pineda & Wiley Enterprises Government Services practice is honored to be a Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB) and to have the opportunity to apply for and fulfill the needs of our government. Both honorably served in the Marine Corps; our founders have a unique passion for continued service long after hanging up the uniform. Our government services practice is ready to deliver exceptional service to our country's critical needs. We understand the rapidly changing demands and commodity requirements necessary to operate efficiently at a high level. As a VOSB, we can deliver commodity and logistical needs while serving as a supervisory and scheduling intermediary with local contractors. This partnership allows you to consolidate all your suppliers and contractors under Pineda & Wiley Enterprises as a VOSB.
