About Us

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises, LLC is an American boutique international trading company that specializes in imports, exports, trade broker services and government services.

Our focus does not stop at providing exceptional service to our clients. We are also passionate about forging strong relationships, both international and domestic. We are a multi-national company currently operating in both the United States and Spain. We are headquartered in the United States in Tempe, Arizona.

We are 100% Veteran Owned and Operated.

Meet the Founders

Cristiane Scott Pineda

Co-Founder & CEO of International Trade and Operations

Cristiane Pineda is both a United States Marines Corps and Operation Enduring Freedom Veteran with years of experience in data analytics, technology and international relations. Born in Fresno, California, Cris has lived and traveled to over 40 countries, which gave him a strong international network of clientele. He is currently based in Madrid, Spain, his roles are focused in both Operations and International Relations.

Adam Ryan Wiley

Co-Founder & CEO of Government Services and Finance

Adam Wiley is a United States Marine Corps Veteran, working within embassy security and data communication operations. Adam has worked on several projects both international and domestic, to include database implementation and applying various data analysis and manipulation tools and techniques to provide exceptional risk advisory and analytics support to clients.
