About Us

Get to know about us

Who are we

Pineda & Wiley Enterprises, LLC is an international trading company that specializes in imports, exports, trade brokering and government services. PWE is 100% Veteran Owned and Operated and are proud to work with the U.S. Federal Government in government to civilian organizational partnerships. Our headquarters is based in Tempe, Arizona, and we work with partners from all over the world, spanning across Latin America to Asia.

What drives us

Excellence is at the pillar of what we do, whether we are working with suppliers trying to expand their reach, securing diverse products for our buyers or import/exporting goods for our own partners or stores. Our standards involve operating at a high level of professionalism, exceeding quotas with unmatched skill.

Work with us

We are a group of young and adventurous entrepreneurs. Our international experience has brought us to all corners of the globe and our love for cultural diversity has helped us forge strong, influential relationships. If you are interested in joining our professional network, please reach out to our team.

Meet the founders

Cristiane Scott Pineda

Co-Founder & CEO of International Trade and Operations

Our partner Cristiane Pineda served in the United States Marine Corps and Operation Enduring Freedom before transitioning out of the armed services and into the technology sector. After completing his undergraduate program at CSU Fresno, Cris attended a graduate program at the reknown IE University in Spain, where he then went on to work as a data scientist. During this time, he procured years of experience in applying his data analytics and technology expertise to international business and relations.

Cristiane has further established himself in the global business sector through unwavering knowledge of various cultures and languages. Being multilingual has provided him with a more personal and unique experience, having traveled to over 40 countries and a robust international network of clients and professionals. He has a deep passion for building lasting relationships and going above and beyond to ensure our client's demands are exceptionally met. His role currently oversees all our international relations and trade operations out of Madrid, Spain.

Adam Ryan Wiley

Co-Founder & CEO of Government Services and Finance

Our partner Adam Wiley served in the United States Marine Corps and Operation Inherent Resolve, primarily focusing on embassy security and data communication operations. After honorably departing the armed services, he completed his undergraduate degree from Arizona State University with a Bachelor of Science in Business Data Analytics with a passion for supply chain operations and analytics. After graduating, he worked as a consultant applying various data analytics tools and techniques. He was able to provide exceptional risk advisory and analytics support to clients across multiple industries.

Adam continues to employ his passion for leveraging data by finding solutions to international trade and global logistics. In this, he is able to improve supply chains and uniquely connect buyers and suppliers to previously unattainable markets. His role currently oversees all of our government services and financial operations in Tempe, Arizona.
